A full address should be beneficial. Be sure to include parking and other helpful information in the event description above.

Your pledge goal should be the minimum amount needed to complete the event to fulfill community service.

This section will declare how many hours a volunteer will accumulate upon finishing the event. Hours will be checked off by the event creator after the duration of the event.
